About Me

A Nursing student turned techy. In the middle of pursuing nursing school Paolo decided that nursing is not for him. Instead decided to jump into tech by going to Lincoln Technical Institute in Edison, NJ for Networking and Communications. After finishing school he immediately got hired at Summit Medical Group as a help desk analyst which jump-started his career in Tech.

With that experience, he continues to land similar jobs within the Application installer, project manager, and technical trainer roles. Now, he is striving to be more involved in creating web applications because of his experience in supporting a wide variety of web applications. Find out more about Paolo through this page as he progresses through the Rutgers Web Development Program.
